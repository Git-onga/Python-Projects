Semester Registration Deadline Extended

October 5, 2023 Registrar's Office

The deadline for semester registration has been extended to Friday, October 15th to accommodate all students who have been experiencing technical difficulties with the online registration portal.

This extension applies to all undergraduate and postgraduate students across all faculties. The university administration recognizes the challenges some students have faced with the new registration system and wants to ensure everyone has ample time to complete their registration.

Important Notes:

  • The portal will remain open until midnight on October 15th
  • Late registration penalties will be waived during this period
  • Technical support will be available extended hours

Students who have already registered need not take any action. For those still needing to register, please ensure you have:

  1. Cleared all outstanding fees
  2. Selected your preferred units
  3. Printed your registration slip for records

For any registration assistance, please visit the ICT helpdesk at the administration block or email registration@kyu.ac.ke.
